Our Work

A department that deals with the legal aspects of weights and measures and the offence committed in a transcation:

The department of Legal Metrology Kashmir is a frontline organisation working for the cause of consumers and enforces various Acts and Rules. The Legal Metrology Act is the principle act enforced by the department, besides this Consumer Proetcion Act, 1986 and various Control Orders are also enforced by the department through proper Government notifications
